William Lawson's

Today whe are trying William Lawson’s blended scotch whisky. William Lawson’s seems to be a brand used by Bacardi to sell blended whisky.

Today we are trying the most common expression of William Lawson’s, which is a NAS. There doesn’t seem to be many more versions, but there is currently an aged 13 years available.

There is no much information about this whisky. It is heavily promoted for cocktails, and taking into account its cheap price we can’t expect to have much information available.

This whisky is bottled at 40% ABV.

Smell notes: Caramel, toffe, sweet, medium alcohol. Taste notes: Alcoholic, caramel.

The nose is better than the taste on this one, although its nothing special either. There are better cheap NAS so I wouldn’t recommend this one.
